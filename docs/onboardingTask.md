## Task Description: 
You have three important things to know. 
1.  YOU CAN APPROVE OR REJECT TICKET REQUESTS LIKE THIS ONE. Click the "Approve" button to approve it. (If you reject, go check the "ALL" tab to see it again. Please note that once you reject a ticket, you can't approve it again.) 
2.  TO GET PAID, YOU NEED TO CONNECT STRIPE. Go to the "EARNINGS" tab to connect your stripe account. 
3.  TO MANAGE YOUR QUEUES (PERSONAL AND PRIORITY) GO TO THE "QUEUES" TAB. Please note that you can only enable the Priority queue if your stripe account is connected.

There are more features to this app, but these are the essentials.
